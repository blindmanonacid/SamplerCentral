Troubleshooting



There is no sound...



...but the knobs/sliders/keys are responding


- There is a bug in SuperCollider in OSX 10.6 that crashes the sound if the headphone output is plugged/unplugged while it is running. Try restarting the program.


- Check if there is sound in the application itself on the level meter. Click somewhere on the menu to get it in focus and then push 'L' on your keyboard.


...and the knobs/sliders/keys are not responding


- Be sure you connected the midikeyboard first, then started Sampler Central


- Check that you got the correct MIDI channel in preferences.
